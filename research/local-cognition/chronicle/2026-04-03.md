**Why:** Voice with confidence-modulated prosody is PS Bot's key differentiator. Needed to map what exists, what's novel, and what stack to use.
**Means:** Major findings: (1) Chatterbox Turbo (MIT, 24k stars) is the only open-source TTS with a programmatic emotion intensity param — direct path to confidence mapping.
