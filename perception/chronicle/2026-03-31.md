## 21:30 — [Decision] Dual perception with principled reconciler

**What:** Two perception channels run simultaneously — visual cortex (screenshot + Set-of-Mark) and analytical cortex (DOM/a11y tree). Disagreements are treated as cognitive dissonance, not errors.
**Why:** No existing framework handles disagreement between visual and analytical perception principally. They all just pick one. The arbitration rule: visual wins for "looks right?", analytical wins for "is clickable?"
**Means:** This is one of 8 claimed novel contributions. Unpublished in the literature.
